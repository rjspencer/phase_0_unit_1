Pairing Session 1

No one has a constant stream of thoughts. By pairing, we can code faster, check eachothers work, and learn from the techniques of others

Pairing also adds a social aspect to a traditionally lonely pursuit.

The extra set of eyes helps prevent traveling down rabbit holes. Your partners can help you realize when to stop and find a different way.

Having someone depending on you forces you to find a solution.

Senior programmers can pair with junior programmers and a mentoring relationship develops. Even peers likely know something you don't know and vice versa. Pair programming is constantly improving the skills of all parties.

Driver/Navigator Advantages

  • Navigator can focus on code/direction instead of typing.
  • Navigator can identify typos more easily.
  • Driver can type faster when he doesn't have to focus on coming up with ideas.
  • Both parties push each other push each other to work harder, better, faster, stronger.

Reflections

I like the comraderie that is pairing. Sometimes I like to be alone, and I will continue to code alone, but I would be fine if the majority of my work was done in pairs.
